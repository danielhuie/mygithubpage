As a recent graduate from the University of Washington with a B.S. degree in Informatics, I have a unique perspective of the world. Coupled with my passion in video production and photography, I believe that it is important to curate digital content to emphasize the need for visual storytelling.

My passion for video first started at The Driver Training Group. I started out as a video editor for the fleet division working with Allstate as their client at the time. I was quickly promoted to lead Videographer where I was able to travel across the United States and film and edit promotional videos highlighting the Safe Driving Challenge events.

My interest in photography came from video production. I wasn't until my Sophomore year of College where I was looking for part-time video work during the school year with UW Student Life. Unfortunately, they didn't have any positions available but they offered me a volunteer position as a school photographer. I gladly took the opportunity and was then able to photograph athletic events and Registered Student Organization events.

As for my technical background, I am a front-end web developer. With data becoming an integral part in app development, it is interesting to see how visual interfaces make use of the data to satisfy the customer's needs and interactions. Starting at the end of August 2016, I will be making my way down to Portland, OR where I will be starting as a Web Developer at Nike WHQ. I am more than excited for what's in store for me at Nike as I continue to live out my passions in digital art and the outdoors.

Complementing my creative lifestyle, I am an avid runner, hiker, and pianist. I love exploring the urban outdoors. If you would like to share a story with me or just grab a cup of coffee, please send me a message!
